About TriniMangaTards

TriniMangaTards is the heart of the manga community in Trinidad & Tobago. We bring the vibrant world of manga to our shores, offering everything from classic series to the latest releases. Our space is more than just a store; it's a gathering place for fans to connect and share their passion.

Our Mission

Our mission is to foster a thriving manga culture in Trinidad & Tobago. We aim to provide access to a diverse range of manga, host engaging community events that celebrate local talent and international creators, and create a welcoming space where fans can connect and share their love for the art form. We are dedicated to being the premier destination for manga lovers, promoting creativity and the joy of reading within our unique island community.

Our History

TriniMangaTards was born from a simple idea during a rainy Carnival season in 2018: to create a space for local manga enthusiasts. A small group of friends, tired of ordering manga online and waiting weeks, started a monthly meetup at a local coffee shop in Port of Spain. The overwhelming enthusiasm led to the creation of an online store, which quickly expanded to a physical location in Curepe by 2020. Our store has since become a beloved gathering spot, hosting weekly manga discussions, artist workshops featuring local Trini artists, and cosplay events that blend Trini culture with anime flair. We've grown from a humble beginning to a cornerstone of the local manga scene, even hosting island-wide manga art competitions.
