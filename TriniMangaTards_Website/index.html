Welcome to TriniMangaTards!

Trini-Manga-Tards is a popular manga store/library where customers from ages 10 and up can come in and rent or buy mangas, or maybe just stay in and read in our cozy space. We provide a vast collection of genres, from thrilling adventure to intense 18+ romance stories. Our goal is to create a welcoming community for all manga lovers, whether you are a newbie or a die-hard fan our door will always be open. We have special events and weekly manga manga discussions
